What is Chit Chat?

Chit Chat is a friendly place for people off all ages to come and chat or ask any questions to a larger community of people, you could call us a social media site made by 1 person! Remember to stick to the community guidlines when using Chit Chat and hope you have fun! See you around!

Community Guidlines

Here at Chit Chat we like to keep things user friendly and have a 0 tolerance policy. As a Chit Chat user you agree to nto put anything NSFW (not appropriate) or rude content. If you break this rule then your comment will be deleted and you will be given 1 chance and if you break it again you will be banned! This means no swearing, nude pictures, rude or innapropriate comment or anything else innapropriate.

Styling Your Messages

In Chit Chat, we have made it so you can style your messages with HTML tags! Such as to add an image or gif to your post use the image element (shown below) and hit send and ta-da, your post now has an image! The img tag can also support gif files, but not videos, and also rememeber that your src value must be a url (https://example.com/image.png as an example).


<img src="Put image location here">


You can also use the h elements to make your text larger or smaller, as show below, with the biggest starting from h1 all the way to the smallest of h6. You can also see that you have to close them off, otherwise it could cause errors in the code meaning that we would have to take your comment down! As for the actual use of these, simply place our text in between the 2 tags and it will be your wanted size, and any text not in between the tags will default to your normal Chit Chat styling.


<h1> </h1>
<h2> </h2>
<h3> </h3>
<h4> </h4>
<h5> </h5>
<h6> </h6>
